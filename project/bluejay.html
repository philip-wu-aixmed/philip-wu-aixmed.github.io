Overview of AIxURO Heuristic Imager

Urine cytology slides are loaded into and scanned by the AIxURO Heuristic Imager with a disease-specific AI algorithm. The AIxURO Heuristic Imager identifies and scans the regions of taget cells at different layers with the optimal focal surface plane.

Business Goal

The long-term vision of Hscan scan software is to efficiently generate quality digital slide images for research purpose, for further analyzing and identifying potential atypical cells by AIxMed proprietary AI model. Hscan is a software solution to implement AIxMed IP scanning method on partner's scanner hardware platform with brightfield imaging mode, 4x objective lens and 20x objective lens.

Goal of Version 1

Development of cytology scanner for clinical research

  • Implement AIxMed IP technology, heuristic scan method, on HW partner's scanner instrucment by integrating partner's HW API Lib/DLL for controlling scanner platform to produce most focal surface plane of cytology slide images
  • Scanner product is capable for FDA clearance or approval
2022-10-01 ~ 2023-06-30 (software simulation) 2023-07-01 ~ 2024-03-31 (testbed / prototype)

Goal of Version 1.1

  • One click to automatically produce quality images available to AIxURO inference under the efficiency of 10 slides completed within one hour
2024-04-01 ~ 2024-08-31
